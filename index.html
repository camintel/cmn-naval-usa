What We Actually Do

Capacity is the product.

We have stood up sovereign shipbuilding capability in nations that had none. Doing it at home is not our market entry — it is the mission.

Engineering Depth — Brought Ashore

Naval architecture & engineering

Hull form, structural design and accommodation arrangement, with platform systems integrated from the drawing forward — auxiliaries, piping, HVAC.

Multi-material construction

Steel, aluminum, and composite technologies under one industrial discipline.

Combat system integration

Combat management, surveillance and sensor suites, and UAV / UUV integration — the unmanned edge, built in.

Signature management

Radar cross-section and infrared management engineered into the hull, not bolted on.

Integrated logistics & training

Lifecycle cost, reliability, availability and maintainability; technical documentation; and training programs built and delivered until the customer can run them alone.

Integrated surveillance solutions

Persistent maritime surveillance delivered as a system, not a sensor.

Every discipline above has one destination: American yards.

Design → Build → Integrate → Deliver → Sustain. We stay for the service life.
