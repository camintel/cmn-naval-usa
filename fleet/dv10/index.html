Mission Architecture

Designed for Tomorrow's Maritime Battlefield.

Modern maritime defense is becoming distributed, attritable, autonomous, and sensor-rich. DV10 is built for that operating picture: a fast optional-unmanned platform that can extend reach, complicate adversary planning, and preserve human decision advantage.

Human-machine teaming sits at the center of the concept. Operators retain command intent while DV10 platforms maneuver, sense, relay, screen, decoy, and respond across contested littorals.

Autonomy Layer

Optional crew. Remote operation. Autonomous execution.

DV10 is conceived around mission planning, autonomous navigation, sensor fusion, resilient communications, and dangerous mission execution where speed and unmanned persistence change the risk equation.

  • Manned / unmanned configuration
  • Mission planning and remote control
  • Sensor relay and distributed awareness
  • Autonomous navigation in littoral spaces

Swarm Warfare

Dozens of intelligent craft can alter the geometry of a fight.

Instead of relying on one expensive patrol vessel to cover every task, DV10 enables distributed sensors, reconnaissance, decoys, electronic warfare support, target saturation, and force multiplication across a wider maritime picture.
