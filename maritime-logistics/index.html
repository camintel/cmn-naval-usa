Global maritime logistics

The mission does not end when the vessel leaves the yard.

AMT plays a strategic role inside the CMN Naval USA group by managing the movement of vessels, spare parts, and maritime equipment from production to deployment. The goal is simple: reduce handoff risk, preserve schedule confidence, and keep naval programs moving across borders, ports, and operating regions.
